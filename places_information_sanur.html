    • Sanur Beach

    Sanur Beach is a famous tourism resort on the island of Bali. This place is located just east of the city of Denpasar, the capital of Bali. Sanur is in the Municipality of Denpasar.

    Because it has quite calm waves, Sanur beach cannot be used for surfing like Kuta Beach. Not far off Sanur Beach there are also diving and snorkeling locations. Because of its friendly conditions, this diving location can be used by divers of all skill levels.

    Sanur Beach is also known as Sunrise beach as opposed to Kuta Beach. Because of its location to the east of the island of Bali, the beach of Bali is the right location to enjoy the sunrise or sunrise. This makes this tourist place even more interesting, there is even a section on this Sanur beach called Matahari Terbit beach because the scenery at Sunrise is very beautiful when seen from there.
