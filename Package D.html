    Bali 13 Days Ultimate Adventure



    Day 1 : Ubud

    Upon arrival at Bali International Airport, meet and greet with our team and transfer to your hotel in Ubud area. On the way, we will visit an authorized money changer. One hour Balinese Traditional Massage are included upon check in.

    Day 2 : Ubud

    Breakfast at hotel, today we will visit Ubud, first we will visit a Balinese house compound; Tegalalang Rice Terrace; Kintamani include lunch; Taste Luwak Coffee and Ubud Market for souvenirs.

    Day 3 : Ubud

    Depart early morning at 06.30 to north east of Bali. The trip will take you at least 3 hours to Tulamben for USS Liberty. You will start diving from the shore and lunch are included.

    Day 4 : Ubud

    Depart early morning at 7.00 to east Bali. The trip will take you at least 2 hours 30 minutes to Candidasa for Gili Tepekong. You will be on a private boat to Gili Tepekong and lunch are included.

    Day 5 : Gilli Air

    Breakfast at hotel, transfer to Padang Bai for fastboat to Gili Air. Upon arrival you will take a Cidomo to your hotel. Free program

    Day 6 : Gilli Air

    Breakfast at hotel and depart with a local boat to the dive spot. Today you will dive 3x in a different or same spot as you like. Lunch will be served at the hotel. One night dive in one spot included.

    Day 7 : Gilli Air

    Breakfast at hotel and depart with a local boat to the dive spot. Today you will dive 3x in a different or same spot as you like. Lunch will be served at the hotel. One night dive in one spot included.

    Day 8 : Nusa Dua

    Breakfast at hotel, check out to the port with a Cidomo and fast boat to Padang Bai. Lunch in a local restaurant in Padang Bai and depart to Nusa Dua by visiting Kedonganan Fish Market and Padang Padang Beach.

    Day 9 : Nusa Dua

    Breakfast at hotel, transfer to Sanur for a departure to Nusa Penida. You will be on a share boat with other divers. Lunch are included.

    Day 10 : Nusa Dua

    Breakfast at hotel, transfer to Sanur for a departure to Nusa Penida. You will be on a share boat with other divers. Lunch are included.

    Day 11 : Nusa Dua

    Breakfast at hotel, free program at your own leisure.

    Day 12 : Nusa Dua

    Breakfast at hotel, free program at your own leisure. Farewell Dinner and you will be picked up at 18.30 by our guide.

    Day 13 : Departure

    Breakfast at hotel and free at your own leisure until departure time. Transfer to the airport for your next destination.
