    Bali 9 Days Under Water Adventure

    Day 1 : Sanur

    Upon arrival at Bali International Airport, meet and greet with our team and transfer to your hotel in Sanur area. On the way, we will visit an authorized money changer. One hour Balinese Traditional Massage are included upon check in.

    Day 2 : Sanur

    Breakfast at hotel, free program in the morning. Depart in the afternoon to Kedonganan fishmarket Padang Padang Beach and Uluwatu Temple with Kecak Dance upon sunset.

    Day 3 : Sanur

    Depart early morning at 6.00 to north west of Bali. The trip will take at least 4 hours ride to Menjangan Island. You will be on a private boat to Menjangan Island and lunch are included.

    Day 4 : Sanur

    Depart early morning at 06.30 to north east of Bali. The trip will take you at least 3 hours to Tulamben for USS Liberty. You will start diving from the shore and lunch are included

    Day 5 : Tanjung Benoa

    Depart early morning at 07.00 to east Bali. The trip will take you at least 2 hours to Padang Bai for Bloo Lagoon. You will start diving from the shore and lunch are included.

    Day 6 : Tanjung Benoa

    Depart early morning at 7.00 to east Bali. The trip will take you at least 2 hours 30 minutes to Candidasa for Gili Tepekong. You will be on a private boat to Gili Tepekong and lunch are included.

    Day 7 : Tanjung Benoa

    Breakfast at hotel, transfer to Sanur for a departure to Nusa Penida. You will be on a share boat with other divers. Lunch are included.

    Day 8 : Tanjung Benoa

    Breakfast at hotel, today we will visit Ubud, first we will visit a Balinese house compound; Tegalalang Rice Terrace; Kintamani include lunch; Taste Luwak Coffee and Ubud Market for souvenirs.

    Day 9 : Departure

    Breakfast at hotel and free at your own leisure until departure time. Transfer to the airport for your next destination.
