    • Kintamani

    The Kintamani tourism area is in the northeastern part of the island of Bali and is below the Mount Batur caldera, one of the active volcanoes in Bali. Apart from the volcano, in the tourist area of Kintamani tourists can also see a view of a natural lake called lake Batur.

    In addition to natural attractions, in the Kintamani area there are also attractions temple tourism objects, namely Ulun Danu Batur temple. For information for you, Ulun Danu Batur temple and Ulun Danu Beratan temple, two different temple attractions. Because the Ulun Danu Beratan temple is located in the Bedugul tourism area.
