    • Nusa Penida

    Nusa Penida is an island located on the southeastern tip of Bali Island which is included in the Klungkung Regency area. Nusa Penida is one of the islands of the 2 islands in Bali, Nusa Lembongan and Nusa Ceningan, of the three islands, Nusa Penida has the most extensive area. Unlike the tourist areas in Bali, Nusa Penida has many hills and cliffs, so this island is a unique and different tourist destination.

    For those of you who are vacationing in Bali but want to feel the atmosphere is different, then you are highly recommended to visit Nusa Penida Island. Not only hills, in Nusa Penida there are also beautiful beaches with blue sea water surrounded by cliffs and rocks that make the Beach in Nusa Penida a beach that tourists want to visit.
