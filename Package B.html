    Bali 5 Days With Crusie dinner



    Day 1 : Seminyak and Kuta

    Upon arrival at International Airport I Gusti Ngurah Rai, meeting service and greet with our representative, transfer to your chosen hotel. Dinner included.

    Day 2 : Kintamani tours

    After breakfast at your hotel, depart to Tegalalng for rice terrace panorama. Proceed to Kintamani for buffet lunch with a view of Mt. Batur & Lake Batur. On the way to Ubud, you will taste the most expensive coffee in the world. Ubud market will be your last visit. Return to hotel.

    Day 3 : Rafting

    After breakfast at your hotel, depart to Payangan for rafting in Ayung River Ubud. Don’t forget to bring dry clothes and cash for souvenirs. Lunch will be served in Payangan. On the way to Tanah Lot, you will visit souvenir shop. Tanah Lot – a famous temple in the sea with a beautiful sunset. Dinner will be served in a local restaurant (choice: seafood meal, vegetarian or Indonesian food). Return to hotel.

    Day 4 : Sunset Cruise Dinner

    Breakfast at hotel, free program in the morning. Transfer to Benoa harbor at 16.00 for Sunset Cruise Dinner with entertainment. Return to your hotel.

    Day 5 : Departure

    Breakfast at your hotel, free program until noon. Check out and transfer to the airport. End of service.
